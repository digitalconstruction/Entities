identifiable entityc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Entities#IdentifiableEntity

'IdentifiableEntity' is a abstract concept that refers to all the central objects that are discussed in the construction project planning and management. Subclasses of the Identifiable Entities include the 'Activity','Group','Information Object','PhysicalObject',and 'Agent'. Identifiable entities are identified by their unique identifiers and can have their specific classification that is defined by certain classification scheme in the construction domain.
